What Makes It Stick

🤝

Group Challenges

Create or join challenges with friends. 30 days, clear rules, shared accountability. The group energy changes everything.

📸

Photo Check-Ins

Snap proof of your daily habit. Your friends see it, react to it, call you out if you skip. Visual accountability.

🔥

Streak System

Consecutive days tracked with fire. Break your streak? Everyone knows. The shame is the feature.

🏆

Leaderboards

See who is crushing it and who is falling behind. Friendly competition makes discipline fun.

💰

Token Stakes

Put tokens on the line. Complete the challenge, split the pot. Quit early, lose your stake. Real consequences.

🔔

Smart Reminders

"Alex just checked in. You haven't." The right nudge at the right time to keep you accountable.

📊

Progress Analytics

Track your completion rate, best streaks, most consistent days. See how you improve over time.

Why It Wins

01

Social Pressure Works

Research shows you're 65% more likely to achieve a goal with an accountability partner. Loop gives you 5. With tokens on the line. Good luck quitting.

02

Daily Active Ritual

Photo check-ins create a daily habit of opening the app. One check-in takes 10 seconds but drives daily retention through the roof.

03

Challenge Virality

Every challenge needs more friends. Users naturally recruit their group to join. "We're doing 30 days no sugar — you in?" Growth is built into the product.
