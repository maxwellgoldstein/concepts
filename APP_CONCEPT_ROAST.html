The Solution

Features That Slap

🎯

Daily Prompts

5 fresh prompts every day — funny, savage, and weirdly specific. Never the same game twice.

🕘

9pm Reveals

Results drop at 9pm sharp. The anticipation is the product. Everyone checking at the same time.

👻

Anonymous Voting

No one knows who voted for who. Say what you actually think — zero consequences.

🔥

Roast Streaks

Vote every day to keep your streak alive. Miss a day, lose your crown. FOMO as a feature.

💬

Reaction Replies

Winners can post a reaction when they get called out. The comments are half the fun.

👑

Weekly Crowns

End-of-week superlatives. Who got roasted the most? Who dodged every bullet? Trophies forever.

🔔

Push Notifications

"Your friends answered a question about you..." — the notification you cannot ignore.

Why It Wins

01

Curiosity Loop

The notification "Someone answered about you" is the most irresistible push in social apps. Gas proved it. We weaponize it.

02

Daily Ritual

Fixed reveal time creates appointment viewing. Every friend group has a 9pm moment now. Retention built into the clock.

03

Viral by Nature

You literally need friends to play. Every user recruits 5-10 more. The game doesn't work alone — growth is the mechanic.
