The Moat
Why first-mover wins forever

Data Network Effect

Every message scanned trains the model. More users = better manipulation detection. By the time a competitor launches, Shield has analyzed billions of real persuasion attempts.

Personal Vulnerability Map

Shield learns YOUR specific weaknesses over time. It knows you cave to scarcity but ignore guilt trips. This personalization can't be replicated on day one.

Trust Moat

Users give Shield access to their most private communications. That trust takes years to build. Nobody switches their persuasion firewall — the switching cost is intimacy.
