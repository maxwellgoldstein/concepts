The Viral Loop

Why Friends Can't Stop Predicting

Forecast turns your friends into the subject of a prediction market. Making predictions ABOUT someone means talking about them, thinking about them, and forcing them to engage.

🔮

Predictions = Engagement

"Someone predicted you WILL text your ex this week." That notification forces a download. You're the subject of predictions you can't see — unless you join.

📈

Weekly Ritual

Sunday predictions, mid-week check-ins, Friday reveals. Three engagement moments per week that become ingrained in your group's routine. Miss a week and you're out.

🏅

Accuracy = Bragging Rights

Who knows the group best? The accuracy leaderboard creates healthy competition. "I predicted all 3 of Jake's moves perfectly" becomes a flex worth sharing.

Features

What Makes Forecast Stick

01

Sunday Prediction Ritual

Every Sunday, make 3 predictions per friend. Quick, fun, forces you to think about each person's upcoming week.

02

Mid-Week Check-Ins

Friends confirm or deny predictions in real-time. "Did you actually go to the gym?" keeps everyone engaged mid-week.

03

Friday Reveal

Full accuracy breakdown every Friday. Who predicted the group best? Leaderboard bragging rights on the line.

04

Accuracy Streaks

Track consecutive weeks of 80%+ accuracy. The "Oracle" badge for anyone who hits 90%+ three weeks running.

05

Prediction History

"Remember when everyone predicted Sam would fail his diet? Week 1 he proved everyone wrong." Memories built in.

06

Predictability Score

How predictable are you? If friends nail your predictions, you're predictable. If you surprise them, you're chaotic.
