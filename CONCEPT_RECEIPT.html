The Viral Loop

Why Receipts Go Everywhere

Receipt turns your daily communication habits into a shareable artifact. The receipt format is already a meme. Quantified social data is irresistible. Comparing with friends creates conversation that drives downloads.

🧾

The Format IS The Meme

CVS receipt format is already viral. "Here's my social receipt" posted on Instagram stories is instant engagement. The format makes people ask "what app is this?" every single time.

📊

Quantified Self = Addiction

"You texted Jake 47 times today." People are obsessed with data about themselves. Spotify Wrapped proved this works. Receipt does it DAILY. Every day you get new data to share.

🔥

Streaks Drive Retention

Communication streaks with each friend. "You and Maya have texted 43 days in a row." Breaking a streak feels like losing a Snapchat streak. Daily retention built into the core mechanic.

Features

Your Social Life, Itemized

01

Daily Receipt

Every night at midnight, your social receipt prints. Texts, calls, first message, last message. The full breakdown.

02

Friend Compare

Side-by-side receipt comparison with friends. Who's more social? Who texts more? Who's the night owl?

03

Communication Streaks

Daily streak with each friend. "You and Jake have texted 43 days straight." Breaking it feels devastating.

04

Weekly Summary

Spotify Wrapped but weekly. Your most-texted person, total messages, social score trend. Shareable card format.

05

Social Score

Algorithmic score based on variety, frequency, and depth of communication. Flex your score or hide it.

06

Top Contact History

Track who your #1 contact is over time. "Jake was your #1 for 3 weeks, now Maya took over." Relationship data.

07

Group Receipts

Aggregate receipt for your whole friend group. "Squad Goals sent 847 messages today." Group social metrics.
