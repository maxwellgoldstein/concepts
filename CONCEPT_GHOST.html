The Magic Moment

Your best friend texts you an address you don't recognize. You walk there. As you approach, your phone starts glowing. A warmth meter fills. At the exact spot, you hear her voice: "This is where I was standing when I found out I got in. I wanted you to feel what this corner of the world feels like to me now." You're standing on a random sidewalk, crying, hearing her voice come from the ground beneath your feet.

Virality
Why it goes viral
💕
"Where we first met" stories
Couples, friends, families leaving emotional voice notes at meaningful places. The content that emerges is unscriptable, raw, and universally relatable.
🗺
Real-world treasure hunts
Creators chain Ghost messages across cities for scavenger hunts. Walk here, hear a clue, go to the next spot. The physical-digital blend is addictive.
👻
The discovery moment is shareable
Recording someone's face when they find a hidden Ghost message is pure gold content. The surprise + emotion combo is algorithm catnip.
Features
Voices, pinned to the earth
📍
GPS-Pinned Audio
Messages are locked to exact coordinates. Sub-meter precision means you stand where they stood.
🌡
Warmth Indicator
As you approach a Ghost, your phone warms up. Visual and haptic feedback guide you to the exact spot.
🔊
Spatial Audio
Messages get louder and clearer as you approach. With AirPods, the sound comes from the direction of the Ghost.
🔒
Private or Public
Leave Ghosts for specific people, friends only, or the whole world. You control who hears your voice.
⏰
Time Capsules
Set Ghosts to unlock on a future date. Leave a message for your child's 18th birthday at the hospital where they were born.
🌐
Ghost Trails
Chain multiple Ghosts into guided walks. City tours, proposal paths, memory lanes — narrated by real voices.
👻
Anonymous Ghosts
Leave public messages without identity. Confessions, poems, jokes — for strangers to discover.
