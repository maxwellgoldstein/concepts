The Moat
Why first-mover wins forever

Behavioral Baseline

Drift needs months of behavioral data to establish your personal baseline. A competitor can't offer "you're drifting" without knowing where you started. Time itself is the moat.

Values Are Intimate

Users articulate their deepest values to Drift. That vulnerability creates loyalty no feature comparison can break. You don't switch your life alignment tool.

Integration Depth

Drift connects to Calendar, Health, Bank, Screen Time, Location — everything. Each new connection deepens accuracy. Rebuilding that web of integrations is a year-long effort.

Virality
Goes viral because...
01

The alignment score is deeply personal and shareable

"My Creativity alignment is 41%. I said it was my top value. Drift just called me out." — This kind of honest self-reflection gets massive engagement.

02

It creates "wake up" moments

Realizing your life doesn't match your values is profound. People share these moments the way they share personality test results — but this one is actually real data.

03

Couples and friends challenge each other

"Are you actually aligned with your values?" becomes a question friends ask each other. Drift becomes the arbiter. Shared accountability drives organic growth.

Features
Everything Drift does
🎯

Value Setting

📊

Alignment Gauges

Beautiful circular gauges for each value, updated daily. See exactly where you're aligned and where you're drifting.

Morning Forecast

Deep Integrations

Screen Time, Health, Calendar, Banking, Location — all feeding into one truthful picture of your life.
