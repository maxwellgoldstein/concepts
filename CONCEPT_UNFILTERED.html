The Viral Loop

Why This Breaks The Internet

Unfiltered generates inherently shareable content. Every round produces a screenshot-worthy moment — an embarrassing photo, a shocking truth, a hilarious reaction. It's content creation disguised as a game.

📸

Screenshot-Worthy Moments

Every single round produces content people want to share. The photo that almost got shown, the truth that got revealed instead — these become group lore and TikTok content.

😰

Adrenaline Rush Addiction

The 5-second veto countdown is pure adrenaline. "What photo is about to be shown?!" The fear and excitement creates a dopamine loop that keeps groups playing for hours.

👥

Only Works With Friends

You need your friend group to play. It's not fun alone. Every player who joins makes it more chaotic and fun. Pure network effects — the more friends, the more unhinged it gets.

Features

Maximum Chaos, Maximum Fun

01

Photo Roulette

Random photo from your camera roll. 5-second preview to veto. The anticipation is everything.

02

Truth Escape

Don't want to show the photo? Answer a truth question your friends submitted instead. Choose your poison.

03

Reaction Feed

Real-time reactions from the group. Emojis, comments, chaos. Every round becomes a shared memory.

04

Veto Limit

Only 3 vetoes per session. Use them wisely. Running out of vetoes raises the stakes dramatically.

05

Custom Truth Bank

Friends submit truth questions for each other. The more personal, the better. "When was the last time you cried?"

06

Highlight Reel

Best moments saved automatically. Weekly recap of the funniest photos and wildest truths from your group.

07

Dare Mode (Premium)

Third option: complete a dare instead. "Send a screenshot of your last DM" or "Call someone on speaker."
