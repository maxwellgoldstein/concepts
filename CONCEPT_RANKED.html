Why Everyone Downloads This

Ranked weaponizes the most fundamental human desire: wanting to know how others perceive you. The notification "You were ranked #1" is the most clickable thing on a phone.

🏆

The #1 Notification

"You were ranked #1 in Best Dressed by your group." That push notification has a 95%+ open rate. You can see THAT you were ranked, but not by WHO — unless you have the app.

📊

Your Social Score

Over time, you build a profile of how your friends see you. Average ranking across all categories. It's your social reputation, quantified. Addictive to track, impossible to ignore.

💬

Group Chat Fuel

Every 8pm reveal sparks conversation. "WHO ranked me last in Best Cook?!" — the app generates real-world social moments that make people want to join.

Features

What Makes Ranked Irresistible

01

Daily Rotating Prompts

New category every day from fun to deep. "Best dancer?" to "Who would you call at 3am?" Never gets stale.

02

Anonymous Voting

No one knows how you voted. Creates honest, sometimes brutal rankings that spark real conversation.

03

8PM Appointment Reveal

Rankings drop at 8pm every night. Becomes a daily ritual your friend group plans around.

04

Lifetime Stats

Track your average ranking over time. See if you're trending up or down. Your social reputation, visualized.

05

Category Crowns

Earn permanent crowns for categories you dominate. Flex your "Best Advice" crown in your profile.

06

Custom Prompts

Submit your own categories for the group to vote on. "Who would survive a zombie apocalypse longest?"

07

Cross-Group Compare

See how you rank across different friend groups. #1 in Best Cook with college friends, #5 with work friends.
