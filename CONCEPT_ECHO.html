Features
What makes Echo magic
🎤
30-Second Voice Print
Record once. Echo learns your vocal DNA — pitch, rhythm, warmth, cadence — and carries it into every language.
⚡
Sub-300ms Latency
Real-time translation during live calls. No awkward pauses, no robotic delays. Feels like a normal conversation.
🌏
40+ Languages
From Japanese to Swahili. Each language model trained on native speakers for authentic pronunciation.
💬
Async Voice Messages
Record a voice note, send it translated. Recipient can toggle between your original and translated versions.
🔒
Voice Never Leaves Device
Your voice print is encrypted and stored locally. Translation happens in a secure enclave. No one gets your voice.
👥
Group Calls
Five people, five languages, one conversation. Each person hears everyone else in their own language.
🎧
Emotion Preservation
Sarcasm, excitement, frustration, tenderness — Echo reads emotional context and preserves it in translation.
