Core Features

🌅

Morning Briefings

Start every day with a personalized overview — meetings, reminders, weather, and insights tailored to your schedule.

⚡

Proactive Nudges

Not a chatbot waiting for input. Sidekick reaches out when it matters — the right message at the right moment.

🧠

Memory Engine

Remembers every conversation, preference, and pattern. The more you use it, the smarter it gets about you.

📊

Weekly Reviews

Honest reflections on your goals, habits, and patterns. Data-driven coaching that actually moves the needle.

🤝

Relationship Intel

Tracks important dates, conversation context, and follow-ups. Never forget what someone told you again.

🔒

Privacy-First

Your data stays yours. End-to-end encrypted. No training on your personal information. Full data export anytime.

Why It Wins

01

Personal Context Moat

The longer you use Sidekick, the harder it is to switch. Your AI learns your life — that knowledge compounds and becomes irreplaceable.

02

Proactive > Reactive

Every AI assistant waits for you to ask. Sidekick initiates. That flip in model changes the entire value prop.

03

Habit Infrastructure

Morning briefings and weekly reviews create natural daily + weekly touchpoints. Retention is structural, not promotional.
